How to Play

Number 8 is a winning number.

By choosing 1 reel you bet on the outcome the first reel.

By choosing 2 reels you bet on the outcome of first two reels.

By betting on 3 reels you bet on all 3 reels and a chance to win the jackpot

Odds

1 reel: 1 in 10 spins
2 reels: 1 in a 100 spins
3 reels: 1 in a 1000 spins
Jackpot: 1 in 52,000 spins

Payouts

Play 1 reel and result is 8XX:
YOU WIN 8.8 credits.

Play 2 reels and result is 88X:
YOU WIN 88 credits.

Play 3 reels and results is 888:
YOU WIN 888 credits and a chance for The Jackpot game!

The Jackpot

A small portion of each bet goes into the jackpot.
Jackpot game is your lucky card out of deck of 52. Good luck!
